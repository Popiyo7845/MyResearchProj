üöÄ Getting Started

Welcome to Robo Dispense Inventory Management System! This guide will help you navigate and use all features of the application.

üìä Dashboard

The Dashboard provides a quick overview of your inventory status.

What You'll See:

  • Total Items: Shows the total number of different products in your inventory
  • Stock In Today: Number of new products added today
  • Low Stock Items: Products with less than 10 units remaining
  • Recent Activity: Timeline of recent inventory changes

üîî Notifications

Stay informed about important inventory alerts through the notification bell icon in the top-right corner.

You'll Receive Alerts For:

  • üî¥ Critical Alerts: Expired products or items expiring within 7 days
  • üü° Warning Alerts: Products expiring within 8-30 days or low stock items
üí° Tip:

Click the bell icon to view all notifications. The red badge shows the total count of active alerts.

üì¶ Adding New Stock

Follow these steps to add new products to your inventory:

Step 1: Click on "Stock in" in the sidebar
Step 2: Fill in all required fields:
  • Product Name (e.g., "Paracetamol 500mg")
  • Quantity (number of units)
  • Brand (manufacturer name)
  • Expiration Date
  • Manufacturing Date
  • Description (optional details)
Step 3: Click "Add to Stock" button
Step 4: You'll receive a confirmation with the auto-generated Item Code
‚ö†Ô∏è Important:

Make sure the manufacturing date is before the expiration date. The system will reject invalid date ranges.

üìã Managing Inventory

View and manage all your products from the Inventory section.

Inventory Table Columns:

  • Item Code: Unique identifier for each product
  • Item Name: Product name
  • Quantity: Current stock level
  • Status: "In Stock" or "Low Stock" indicator
  • Action: "Out" button to remove stock

Removing Stock (Stock Out):

Step 1: Click the red "Out" button next to the product
Step 2: A modal window will appear showing:
  • Product name
  • Current stock level
  • Input field for quantity to remove
Step 3: Enter the quantity to remove
  • System shows remaining stock in real-time
  • Validates you don't exceed available quantity
  • Warns if removing all stock (product will be deleted)
Step 4: Click "Remove Stock" to confirm
üí° Tip:

You can press ESC or click outside the modal to cancel the operation.

‚úÖ Best Practices

  • Regular Monitoring: Check notifications daily to stay ahead of expiring products
  • Accurate Data Entry: Always double-check dates and quantities when adding stock
  • Maintain Stock Levels: Keep items above 10 units to avoid low stock alerts
  • Update Promptly: Remove stock immediately when products are dispensed
  • Review Dashboard: Start each day by reviewing the dashboard statistics
